About Me

I'm Row, a web developer based out of Napa Valley, near the Bay Area in California. I've always been a creator at heart, making functional and beautiful things with whatever tools I've had available to me, and tinkering around with websites and HTML since the Web 1.0 age. To me, programming is like a craftsperson's toolbox in a digital world; however, unlike their timeless counterparts, they're rapidly changing and I'm always finding exciting new ways to solve problems.

My other hobbies include illustration, fiction writing, and playing video games. Drop me a line!
